The Impact of AI on Modern Journalism

By Eleanor Vance

Pending

Content Excerpt

Artificial intelligence is rapidly transforming the landscape of journalism, offering new tools for data analysis, content generation, and audience engagement. However, concerns about job displacement, algorithmic bias, and the potential for misinformation remain prominent. This article explores the dual nature of AI's influence, highlighting both its promising applications and the ethical dilemmas it presents to media organizations worldwide.
